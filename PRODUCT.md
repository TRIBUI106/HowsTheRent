# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Solo Vietnamese landlords managing 10–50 boarding house rooms. They open the app in the morning to check daily status: overdue invoices, new maintenance requests, who needs attention. They are not tech-savvy power users — they need trust and clarity above all else. Secondary users: tenants checking invoices and submitting maintenance requests; technicians receiving and updating work orders.

## Product Purpose

HowsTheRent digitalises the full operational workflow of a Vietnamese boarding house: contracts, monthly invoicing, maintenance, PayOS online payments, and audit logging. Success means the landlord never misses a payment, never loses track of a maintenance issue, and can hand the system to someone else without training.

## Brand Personality

Grounded, precise, dependable. The tool of a careful operator, not a startup.

## Anti-references

- Generic indigo SaaS: gradient heroes, identical feature card grids, Tailwind-starter-template dark sidebars
- Consumer travel / Airbnb: photography backgrounds, lifestyle warmth, rounded softness
- Cold bank UI: navy + gold, compliance-forward formality, serif headings
- Cheap accounting software: no breathing room, aggressive primary colours, 1990s form density

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity before cleverness.** The landlord needs to know what requires action today. Hierarchy serves that scan, not decoration.
2. **Trustworthy through restraint.** Visual noise erodes trust. Every element must justify its presence.
3. **Operator-grade, not babied.** Dense enough to be efficient; clear enough to need no manual.
4. **Consistent without being monotonous.** Rhythm through spacing variation, not by repeating the same card pattern everywhere.
5. **Vietnamese context first.** Copy, date formats, currency (VND), and cultural register should feel native, not translated.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Support keyboard navigation throughout. Maintain readable contrast for all text. No reliance on colour alone to convey status.
